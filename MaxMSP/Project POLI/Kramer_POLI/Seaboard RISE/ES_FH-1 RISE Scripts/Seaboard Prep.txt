Monophonic:
Connect the Seaboard to your computer by USB and open up ROLI Dashboard.
Select Channel Mode: Single and Channel: 9.
Select Press Mode: Channel Pressure.
Set the XY Touchpad’s outputs to their defaults of CC113 & CC114.
Adjust the Pitch Bend Range to 48.

Duophonic:
Connect the Seaboard to your computer by USB and open up ROLI Dashboard.
Select Channel Mode: Multi, MPE: Off, and a Channel Range of 9–10.
Select Press Mode: Channel Pressure.
Set the XY Touchpad’s outputs to their defaults of CC113 & CC114.
Make sure that the Touch Faders are set to their defaults, CC107, CC109, and CC111.
Adjust the Pitch Bend Range to 48.
